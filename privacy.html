Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Cubiks ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile puzzle game. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party:

  • Game progress (levels completed, star ratings, 34 achievements)
  • Game statistics (play time, moves made, fastest times)
  • Settings preferences (sound, haptics, color blind mode)
  • Cosmetic selections (skins, patterns, styles, effects, backgrounds, sound packs)
  • In-app purchase status
  • Coin balance and rewards earned

Information Collected by Third Parties

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our app. Google may collect:

  • Device advertising identifiers
  • General ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)
  • Approximate location (country/region level) for ad targeting

For more information about Google's data practices, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

3. How Information is Used

Local Data

  • To save your game progress so you can continue where you left off
  • To track your achievements, statistics, and unlocked cosmetics
  • To remember your preferences, settings, and selected customizations

Third-Party Advertising Data

  • To display relevant advertisements
  • To measure ad effectiveness
  • To provide free access to the game

4. Your Choices

App Tracking Transparency

On iOS 14.5 and later, we will request your permission before allowing ad-related tracking. You can change this permission at any time in your device's Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

Remove Ads

You may purchase the "Remove Ads" option within the app to permanently disable interstitial advertisements. Rewarded ads (which you choose to watch for bonuses) will remain available.

Data Deletion

To delete all local game data, simply uninstall the app from your device. All your progress, statistics, and preferences will be permanently removed.

You may also reset your advertising identifier in your device's privacy settings to limit ad tracking.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. The only data sharing that occurs is:

  • Advertising data shared with Google AdMob for the purpose of serving ads
  • Purchase verification with Apple for in-app purchases

6. Children's Privacy

Cubiks is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app:

  • Does not require account creation
  • Does not collect names, emails, or other identifying information
  • Does not include social features or user-generated content
  • Uses age-appropriate advertisements through Google AdMob's child-safe settings

7. Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms. We do not operate servers that store your personal data. Your game progress never leaves your device.

8. International Users

Cubiks is available worldwide. If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or California, we comply with applicable privacy laws including GDPR and CCPA through our consent management platform.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last revised.
