Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Cubiks?

Swipe on rows or columns to rotate the cubes. Your goal is to make all visible cube faces match the target color shown at the top of the screen. The fewer moves you use, the more stars you earn!

What do the stars mean?

You can earn 1 to 3 stars on each level based on how efficiently you solve the puzzle. Solving in fewer moves earns more stars. A "Perfect" solve means you used the exact minimum number of moves possible.

I'm stuck on a level. What can I do?

Tap the hint button (lightbulb icon) to enable the walkthrough feature. This will guide you through the puzzle step by step. You can also use the Restart button to try a fresh approach.

How does Undo work?

You get one free Undo per level. If you need additional undos, you can watch a short video to earn an extra one. The Undo button appears grayed out when no moves have been made yet.

What is Endless Mode?

After completing levels in the main campaign, you can try Endless Mode for infinite procedurally-generated puzzles. Choose between Easy (3x3), Medium (4x4), or Hard (5x5) difficulty.

How do I unlock skins and patterns?

Skins and patterns are unlocked by completing levels, earning stars, achieving milestones, and earning coins. Visit the Skins menu to see unlock requirements for each customization option.

What is Color Blind Mode?

Color Blind Mode adds unique patterns to each cube face color, making it easier to distinguish faces without relying solely on color. Enable it in Settings.

How do I remove ads?

You can purchase the "Remove Ads" option from the Settings menu. This is a one-time purchase that permanently removes interstitial (full-screen) ads. Rewarded ads that give you bonuses will still be available if you choose to watch them.

How do I restore my purchase?

If you previously purchased "Remove Ads" and need to restore it (for example, on a new device), go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Can I transfer my progress to a new device?

Game progress is stored locally on your device. Currently, there is no cloud save feature. If you get a new device, you'll start fresh, but your "Remove Ads" purchase can be restored.

How do I reset my progress?

To completely reset your game, uninstall and reinstall the app. This will erase all progress, statistics, and achievements. Note: Your "Remove Ads" purchase will remain and can be restored.

Tips for Success

  • Think ahead: Plan your moves before swiping. Look at all sides of the cube.
  • Use the tilt button: Tapping "Tilt" lets you see the cube from a different angle.
  • Watch for patterns: Many puzzles have similar solutions. Learn common move sequences.
  • Don't give up: Every puzzle has a solution! Use the walkthrough if you need help.
